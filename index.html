About Me
Why Healthcare IT?

I am a .NET Fullstack Developer dedicated to building the healthcare technology ecosystem in Indonesia. To me, writing code is not just about completing lines of commands — it is about ensuring that Hospital Management Information Systems (HMIS) run seamlessly to protect patient safety and empower medical professionals.

With over 4 years of experience, I am well-versed in tackling critical challenges such as BPJS Kesehatan API integration, Electronic Medical Record (EMR) synchronization via SATUSEHAT (Ministry of Health, Republic of Indonesia), and implementing artificial intelligence for early clinical diagnosis. I believe technology is the key to accelerating Indonesia's national healthcare services.

My Work Principles
  • High Reliability: Medical systems must run 24/7 with zero tolerance for failure.
  • Data Security: Maximum protection for patient medical records in compliance with national standards.
  • Process Efficiency: Automating registration & care workflows so doctors can focus on patients.
